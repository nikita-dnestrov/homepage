Résumé

— I am not composed altogether of flesh and blood. I have shape but that shape is not made of matter. Although I have never lived, I could never really die. When the stars were right, I could plunge from world to world through the sky; but now that the stars are wrong I lay in the deepest slumber under the great city of R'lyeh, waiting for a glorious resurrection when the stars and the earth might once more be ready for me.
